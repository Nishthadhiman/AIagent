Niche Expertise: Education & Skill Centers

Scale Your Enrollments with AI Education Agents

Turn student inquiries into admissions. Our AI agent handles course details, demo bookings, and follow-ups 24/7.

In the education sector, high-volume inquiries are common, especially during admission seasons. For most coaching centers, the manual task of calling back every student who fills out a form is overwhelming. A delayed response often means the student joins a competitor. Our specialized Education AI Voice Agent solves this by providing instant, human-like follow-ups to every single inquiry.

Instant Lead Nurturing for Institutes

Our AI agents are designed to handle student and parent psychology. They act as a 24/7 virtual counselor, answering questions about course duration, fees, faculty, and batch timings. Built with Advanced Natural Language Processing, the AI identifies the student's interest level and can instantly book a seat for a demo class or a counseling session, ensuring no lead is ever wasted.

Education-Specific AI Features:

  • Instant Demo Booking: Automatically schedules students for free demo sessions.
  • Multilingual Counseling: Support for regional languages to help parents understand the value.
  • n8n Enrollment Sync: Syncs lead data with your CRM or LMS (Learning Management System).
  • Automated Reminders: Sends WhatsApp/Call reminders for upcoming classes or exams.

Deep Automation with n8n Workflows

The core of our solution is n8n automation. When a student talks to the AI, their intent is captured and analyzed. If they show high interest, the AI can instantly trigger an email with the course brochure or a WhatsApp message with the payment link. This end-to-end automation allows your teaching staff to focus on education while the AI handles the business side of enrollment.
