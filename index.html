Common Questions

What exactly is an AI Agent?
An AI Agent is a specialized software program that uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to perform specific tasks autonomously, such as talking to customers, searching for data, or managing emails without human intervention.
How does the AI Calling Agent work?
Our calling agents use advanced Voice AI to conduct real-time conversations. They can understand intent, answer questions, and qualify leads before pushing the data to your CRM.
Is the voice quality natural or robotic?
We use the latest text-to-speech technology that ensures a human-like tone, including natural pauses and inflections, making the conversation feel professional and fluid.
Which industries can benefit from these agents?
While our agents are versatile, they excel in Real Estate, Insurance, Solar Energy, SaaS, and Digital Marketing Agencies where lead prospecting is crucial.
What is n8n automation?
n8n is a powerful workflow automation tool. We use it to connect your AI agents with over 200+ apps like Google Sheets, HubSpot, Slack, and WhatsApp for a seamless experience.
How does the Backlink Finder help in SEO?
Our SEO agent automatically scans thousands of websites to find high-authority guest post opportunities and competitor backlink gaps, saving you hundreds of hours of manual work.
Can I integrate these with my current CRM?
Absolutely. We can integrate our agents with any CRM that has an API, including GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, and Zoho.
Is my business data secure with AI?
Yes. We prioritize data privacy. All AI workflows are designed to handle data securely, and we do not use your proprietary business data to train public AI models.
How long does it take to set up an agent?
A basic setup typically takes 3-5 business days. More complex custom workflows with deep CRM integrations may take up to 2 weeks.
Do I need technical knowledge to use this?
Not at all. We handle the technical setup and deployment. You will receive a simplified dashboard or direct lead notifications via email or Slack.
